2. Core Data Policy: Zero Collection & Local Storage

BudgetMan is designed fundamentally for security and user control. We commit to a policy of Zero External Data Collection. This means:

  • NO BANK LINKING: The App does not require, request, or connect to any external financial accounts, bank logins, or credit card details.
  • LOCAL DATA STORAGE: All user-inputted financial data (expenses, income, budgets, categories, and transaction history) is processed and stored **exclusively on the user's local device**.
  • NO EXTERNAL TRANSMISSION: The Developer **does not upload, sync, or transmit** your sensitive financial data to any external servers, cloud services, or third parties. Your financial life remains entirely private and on-device.

3. Data Processing and Usage (On-Device Only)

The App processes data strictly on your local device to provide core functionality:

Data Processed Locally:

  • User-Inputted Financial Data: Transactions, income, custom categories, and financial goals.
  • App Usage Data: Non-personal technical logs are generated to monitor app stability and performance (e.g., crash reports).

Purpose of Processing:

  1. Core Functionality: To calculate account balances, generate financial reports, and display spending analytics.
  2. AI-Powered Insights: The integrated AI system automatically analyzes the **locally stored user data** to generate personalized Smart Alerts and financial suggestions. This processing is performed **entirely on your device** without any data being sent to the Developer.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share your private financial information with any third parties for any purpose, including marketing or profiling.

  • Financial Data Sharing: **NONE.** Your expense and income data are not shared, sold, or disclosed externally.
  • Third-Party Services: We may utilize standard, privacy-focused analytical tools (e.g., Google Firebase for crash reporting) to improve app stability. These tools are configured to collect **anonymous, non-personal data only** and are not linked to your financial records.

5. Data Retention and User Control (Right to Erasure)

You have full, immediate control over your data:

  • Data Retention: Data is retained only for as long as the App remains installed and actively used on your device.
  • Data Deletion: You can **instantly and permanently** delete all of your data by using the "Clear All Data" function within the App's settings, or by simply **uninstalling the App**. Since no server copy exists, the deletion is final upon removal from your device.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we make any material changes to our data handling practices (for example, if we were to introduce optional server-side syncing), we will notify users clearly through the App or the Google Play Store listing before such changes take effect.
